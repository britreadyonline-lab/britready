Study with structure

BritReady is designed for people preparing for the Life in the UK Test who want short practice sessions, realistic mock tests, and a clear picture of readiness before exam day.

Practice questions

Work through Life in the UK style questions and explanations in focused sessions.

Mock tests

Take timed mock tests with the same 24-question, 45-minute rhythm learners expect.

Progress tracking

See scores, streaks, and weaker areas so revision time goes where it matters.

Bookmarks

Save difficult questions and come back to them before test day.

Weak area practice

Spend more time on topics that need attention instead of guessing what to revise next.

Daily reminders

Optional local reminders help learners build a steady practice habit.

Independent preparation app

BritReady is an independent study tool. It is not an official UK Government app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the UK Government.

Important: BritReady is for test preparation only. Learners should always check official government guidance for current Life in the UK Test rules, booking information, fees, and eligibility requirements.
