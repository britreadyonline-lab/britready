Data Deletion

Instructions for removing local app data or contacting us about a data request.

No Required Account

BritReady does not currently require users to create an account. Most app data, such as progress, bookmarks, settings, and practice history, is stored locally on your device.

Delete Local App Data

  1. Delete the BritReady app from your device, or clear the app data from your device settings where supported.
  2. Restart your device if your operating system recommends it.
  3. Reinstalling the app may start a fresh local app state, unless your device or store account restores app data.

Request Help

For privacy or data deletion questions, email britreadyonline@gmail.com with the subject line "BritReady Data Deletion Request". Include the device platform you use, such as iOS or Android, and a brief description of your request.

Purchases and Store Records

Purchase, subscription, and refund records are managed by Apple App Store, Google Play, and related payment or entitlement providers. You may need to manage subscriptions, receipts, or billing records through the store account used for purchase.
