BritReady

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Overview

BritReady is a Life in the UK Test preparation app. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and how to contact us about privacy requests.

Contact: britreadyonline@gmail.com

Information We Collect

BritReady may collect or process the following types of information:

  • App usage information, such as screens viewed, features used, practice activity, and general performance events.
  • Crash and diagnostic information, such as device type, operating system version, app version, error logs, and crash traces.
  • Purchase and entitlement information handled through app store billing and RevenueCat, such as subscription or lifetime access status.
  • Advertising-related information handled by Google Mobile Ads, where ads are shown in the app.
  • Local app data stored on your device, such as progress, bookmarks, settings, and practice history.
  • Messages you send us by email, including your email address and the contents of your request.

BritReady does not currently require users to create an account.

How We Use Information

  • To provide app features such as practice sessions, mock tests, progress tracking, bookmarks, and settings.
  • To understand and improve app performance and user experience.
  • To diagnose crashes, bugs, and technical issues.
  • To manage premium access, subscriptions, purchase restoration, and entitlements.
  • To show ads where applicable and comply with advertising platform requirements.
  • To respond to support, privacy, and data deletion requests.

Third-Party Services

BritReady uses third-party services that may process information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Analytics for app analytics.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics.
  • Google Mobile Ads for advertising.
  • RevenueCat for purchase entitlement management.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play for app distribution and billing.

Notifications

BritReady may offer optional study reminders using local notifications on your device. You can manage notification permissions in your device settings.

Data Retention

Local progress, settings, and bookmarks may remain on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. Third-party analytics, crash, advertising, and purchase records may be retained by the relevant service providers according to their policies and legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.
  • You can control notification permissions in your device settings.
  • You can manage subscriptions and purchases through the Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • You can contact us at britreadyonline@gmail.com with privacy questions or data requests.

Children

BritReady is intended for people preparing for the Life in the UK Test and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
