Common Questions

How do I restore purchases?

Use the restore purchase option in the app if available. Purchases are tied to the Apple ID or Google Play account used at checkout.

How do I manage subscriptions?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings.

How do I delete local progress?

You can delete the app or clear app data on your device. For additional requests, contact us by email.
